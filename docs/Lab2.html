upper_bound = Quartile_3 + 1.5 * Interquartile_range

# Remove outliers

My inferences¶

Second Approach that is Using IQR for outlier dectection is best because¶

In IQR technique the mean is close to median and when the mean is close to the median, it indicates a balanced distribution without a significant skew. In IQR technique the median is exactly the same as mode and when they are equal, it suggests a balance in the distribution around the central tendency. Based on the comparison, the IQR (Whiskers) Approach seems to be more effective in handling outliers and providing a central tendency measure less influenced by extreme values.
